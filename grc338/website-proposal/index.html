Introduction

That's the Stuff

This store is located in San Luis Obispo, California and is known for its delicious pastries that are stuffed with a variety of sweet fillings. Specifically, the shop features unique ice cream donut sandwiches, taiyaki, and mochi donuts that are guaranteed to satisfy sweet cravings.

Target audience

That’s the Stuff aims to attract sweet tooths of all ages, but one of the main target audiences are college students. Since many college students crave a late-night snack, the store will be open until 1:00am and will provide delivery services.

Anyone craving a sweet treat at an affordable price would visit this site to explore the menu and learn about our operating hours, location, and delivery services.

About Us

This is a story all about how our ice cream got flipped turned upside down and I'd like to take a minute so scoop on by I'll tell you how we became "That’s the Stuff" started by two guys.

With little to no culinary background, besides the occasional cup noodles here and there, owners Jackie and Alan were able to whip up some "spot on" flavors and fillings along with warm fluffy donuts. We strive for every single flavor to taste true to their name. After months of extensive research and experimentation, we perfected deserts filled with delicious ingredients so you can say, “That’s the Stuff!”
